Introduction

Signal Magazine

Signal is a digital magazine focused on the role of sound design in user experience. The publication explores how audio shapes the way people interact with digital products. This can cover a range of topics where sound design is relevant, such as music streaming platforms, mobile apps, games, AI tools, and notifications. Instead of covering music at large, Signal focuses specifically on the interaction aspect of sound for digital products: clicks, alerts, transitions, silence, feedback noises, and AI-assisted audio experiences. Through analysis, interviews, and visual storytelling, the magazine teaches readers how sound affects user behavior and emotion, and its role in making interfaces feel more intuitive.
